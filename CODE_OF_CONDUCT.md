# Code Of Conduct

Private AI Platform Kit follows a simple contributor standard: be respectful, direct, and focused on improving the project.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and professional language.
- Respect different experience levels and operational contexts.
- Give feedback that is specific, actionable, and tied to project goals.
- Keep security-sensitive details out of public issues and discussions.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private customer data, secrets, prompt content, or exploit details.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions after maintainers have redirected them.
- Misrepresenting sample evidence as production proof.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers at [conduct@fluentorbit.de](mailto:conduct@fluentorbit.de). Reports are handled confidentially, and the reporter's identity is not shared outside the maintainers without consent. Do not include secrets, customer data, or private prompt content in a report.

Vulnerabilities and other security-sensitive issues follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md), not this address.

## Enforcement

Maintainers acknowledge conduct reports within a few business days. Depending on severity, maintainers may warn the participant, edit or remove comments, close issues, block participants, or report abuse to the hosting provider.
